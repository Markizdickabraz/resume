About me

Hello, my name is Mark. I'm an experienced sales manager and supervisor with over 6 years of expertise in distributions. However, my true passion lies in the world of technology and coding. After delving into HTML and CSS on my own and discovering my affinity for coding, I decided to pursue a career in IT.

To enhance my skills further, I joined an IT course where I not only learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Figma, and GIT but also took on leadership roles in team projects. With a drive to excel, I consistently stayed ahead of the curriculum, determined to complete the course in the shortest possible time.

Now, I'm thrilled about the opportunity to work with a team of intelligent individuals who are dedicated to building modern, impactful products. I'm ready to contribute my hard skills in adaptive, responsive, and cross-browser layout design to create cutting-edge technologies and achieve our collective goals.
