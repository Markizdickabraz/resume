Про мене

Hello, my name is Mark. With over 6 years of experience in distributions, I have honed my skills as a sales manager and supervisor. However, I have always felt a strong inclination towards the world of technology and coding. The idea of venturing into IT had been brewing in my mind for quite some time, but it wasn't until I made a second attempt in a more mature age that I realized it was truly my passion. My journey into the realm of coding began with self-study. I immersed myself in online resources, devouring knowledge on HTML and CSS. As I delved deeper, I became captivated by the art of creating cutting-edge technologies. The more I learned, the more I craved an environment where I could apply and structure the knowledge I had acquired. Motivated by this desire, I made the decision to seek new opportunities that would allow me to grow further. I enrolled in an IT course, eager to expand my skill set, network with like-minded individuals, and unleash my full potential. Driven by my determination, I quickly progressed through the modules, often staying a few steps ahead of the curriculum. During the course, I had the opportunity to collaborate on team projects, where I not only demonstrated my technical prowess but also showcased my leadership skills. Guiding and motivating my team, I ensured the successful completion of our projects. These experiences solidified my excitement about working alongside intelligent and knowledgeable individuals who share a passion for building modern, impactful products. In terms of hard skills, I have a strong command of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Figma, and Git. I am proficient in creating adaptive, responsive, and cross-browser layouts, ensuring seamless user experiences across different devices and platforms. Now, standing at the intersection of my sales management background and my newfound expertise in web development, I am poised to embark on an exciting new chapter. I am eager to leverage my skills, collaborate with talented individuals, and contribute to the creation of innovative solutions that leave a lasting impact. I am wholeheartedly committed to doing whatever it takes to achieve these goals and make meaningful contributions to the team and the industry as a whole.
